## 6. SUCCESS METRICS

### PRIMARY METRICS (Track daily):
- **Link Clicks** – Total clicks to landing page/Gumroad (diagnostic for ad appeal)
- **Cost Per Click (CPC)** – Total spend ÷ clicks (target: <$1.50; X average is $0.50-$2.00)
- **Conversions** – Number of $39 template purchases (tracked via pixel)
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CAC)** – Total spend ÷ conversions (target: <$15 to maintain 2.6x ROAS)
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** – Revenue ÷ ad spend (target: >2:1 to be profitable)

### SECONDARY METRICS:
- **Impressions** – Total ad views (diagnostic for reach and frequency)
- **Engagement Rate** – Likes, replies, retweets ÷ impressions (diagnostic for messaging resonance)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** – Link clicks ÷ impressions (benchmark: 0.5-2% for X ads)
- **Conversion Rate** – Conversions ÷ link clicks (diagnostic for landing page quality)

### DECISION THRESHOLDS:
- **PAUSE** if CAC >$20 or ROAS <1.5:1 (unprofitable)
- **OPTIMIZE** if CPC >$2.00 (reduce bid or refine targeting)
- **SCALE** if ROAS >3:1 and CAC <$12 (increase daily budget by 20-30%)
- **TEST CREATIVE** if CTR <0.5% (current ad isn't resonating; try image/video)

| Aspect | Reddit | X (Twitter) |
