## My Actual Recommendation

**This is a solid framework for a flat-budget scenario. Execute it in phases.**

### Phase 1: Audit Current Ad Spend (Weeks 1-2)

**Analyze performance by channel** [1][2][5][7]:
- Document ROAS for each channel (Google, Meta, TikTok, etc.)
- Document CPA for each channel
- Identify underperformers (ROAS <1.5x or CPA above target)
- Identify top performers (ROAS >3x or CPA below target)

**Success criteria:**
- You have clear visibility into which channels are profitable
- You've identified at least 20% of spend that's underperforming
- You know your acquisition vs. retargeting split

**Output:**
A spreadsheet with:
| Channel | Current Spend | ROAS | CPA | Status |
|---------|---------------|------|-----|--------|
| Google Ads | $X | Y | $Z | Keep/Cut/Optimize |
| Meta | $X | Y | $Z | Keep/Cut/Optimize |
| TikTok | $X | Y | $Z | Keep/Cut/Optimize |

### Phase 2: Reallocate Budget (Weeks 3-4)

**Pause underperformers** [1][2][5][7]:
- Cut campaigns with ROAS <1.5x
- Pause non-converting keywords
- Redirect budget to top performers

**Implement automation** [1][3]:
- Set up machine learning bidding (Google Ads)
- Allocate 10-20% for testing new formats/audiences
- Keep total spend flat

**Rebalance acquisition vs. retargeting** [3]:
- Shift to 70% acquisition / 30% retargeting
- Build long-term LTV pipeline
- Reduce short-term retargeting spend

**Result:**
- Same total ad spend
- Higher ROAS (by cutting waste)
- "Virtual" budget freed for organic

### Phase 3: Activate Organic Growth (Weeks 5-8)

**Prioritize SEO** [3]:
- Audit current SEO performance
- Identify high-intent keywords you're not ranking for
- Create/optimize content for those keywords
- Timeline: 2-3 months to see results

**Repurpose content** [7]:
- Audit existing blogs, videos, posts
- Identify top performers
- Repurpose for social media, email, Google My Business
- Low effort, high visibility

**Build retention channels** [3][7]:
- Segment email list by customer value
- Create personalized messaging sequences
- Implement SMS for high-value customers
- Track repeat purchase rate

**Maintain consistent effort** [6]:
- Don't cut marketing activities
- "Historical data shows maintainers outperform cutters"
- Continue audience-targeted strategies
- Gain share while competitors cut

### Phase 4: Monitor and Optimize (Ongoing)

**Weekly tracking** [1][2]:
- Monitor ROAS/CPA benchmarks
- Track organic traffic growth (SEO, content)
- Track repeat purchase rate (retention)
- Adjust paid spend if organic ramps slower than expected

**Monthly review:**
- Compare organic growth vs. paid growth
- Identify which organic channels are working
- Double down on winners
- Pause underperformers
