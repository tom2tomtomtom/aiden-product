## PREMIUM TIER PROMOTION CAMPAIGN

### 1. CAMPAIGN CONCEPT (ONE LINE)

**"Unlock premium: Personalized upgrade offers + exclusive access + urgency for existing customers → drive tier migrations in 4 weeks."**

---

### 2. TARGET AUDIENCE (SPECIFIC)

**Primary: Existing customers segmented by upgrade potential**

**Segment 1: High-Engagement Free Users (40% of base)**
- Frequent product usage (logged in 3+ times/week)
- High interaction with features (clicks, time spent)
- Likely to convert (already understand value)
- **Why:** Most ready to upgrade, lowest friction[5][6]

**Segment 2: Mid-Tier Users (35% of base)**
- Currently on basic paid tier
- Show interest in premium features (clicked learn more, watched demo)
- High lifetime value potential
- **Why:** Easiest upsells, highest LTV lift[5]

**Segment 3: Lapsed Users (15% of base)**
- Haven't used product in 30+ days
- Previously engaged (prove they saw value)
- Need re-engagement + incentive
- **Why:** Win-back opportunity, lower cost than new acquisition[5]

**Segment 4: Power Users (10% of base)**
- Already using premium features
- High engagement, high spending
- Candidates for higher-tier premium
- **Why:** Highest LTV, easiest to upgrade[5]

**Key insight:** Don't send same offer to all. Personalize by segment and usage behavior.[5][6]

### 3. CHANNELS AND TACTICS

**CHANNEL 1: EMAIL (70% reach)**[5]

**Tactic 1a: Personalized Email Sequence (Days 1-7)**[5][6]

**Email 1 (Day 1): "You're missing out" - Personalized value prop**

Subject: "[Name], you're using [feature] but missing [premium feature]"

Body:
"Hi [Name],

I noticed you've been using [specific feature] a lot—great taste.

But here's what you're missing: [Premium feature that solves their pain point].

It would save you [X hours/week] and unlock [specific benefit].

For the next 7 days, upgrade to premium for [X% off or $X/month].

[CTA: Upgrade Now]

[Your name]"

**Key elements:**[5][6]
- Reference their specific usage (personalization)[5][6]
- Highlight premium feature they're missing[5]
- Show concrete benefit (time saved, problem solved)[5]
- Limited-time offer (7 days)[5]
- Clear CTA[5]

**Email 2 (Day 4): Social proof - "Others like you upgraded"**

Subject: "[Name], [X]% of users like you upgraded to premium"

Body:
"Hi [Name],

Quick update: [X]% of customers using [feature] like you have upgraded to premium in the last 3 days.

Here's why they said yes:
- '[Quote from customer testimonial]'
- '[Quote from customer testimonial]'
- '[Quote from customer testimonial]'

Your offer expires in 3 days. [CTA: Upgrade Now]

[Your name]"

**Key elements:**[5]
- Social proof (others upgraded)[5]
- Customer testimonials (trust)[5]
- Urgency (3 days left)[5]
- Clear CTA[5]

**Email 3 (Day 7): Final push - "Last chance"**

Subject: "[Name], your upgrade offer expires today"

Body:
"Hi [Name],

This is your last chance to upgrade at [X% off / $X/month].

After today, the offer is gone.

[CTA: Upgrade Now]

[Your name]"

**Key elements:**[5]
- Urgency (last day)[5]
- Simple, direct message[5]
- Clear CTA[5]

**Send to:** All segments (personalized by segment)[5]

**CHANNEL 2: IN-APP NOTIFICATIONS (Immediacy)**[5]

**Tactic 2a: Contextual In-App Prompts (Days 1-28)**[5]

**Trigger 1: When user tries premium feature (as free user)**
- Message: "Unlock [feature] with premium. [X% off for 7 days]. [CTA: Upgrade]"
- Timing: Immediately when they hit paywall
- Expected conversion: 10-15% (high intent)[5]

**Trigger 2: After 10+ sessions in 7 days**
- Message: "You're a power user. Premium is made for you. [X% off]. [CTA: Upgrade]"
- Timing: After they've proven engagement
- Expected conversion: 8-12%[5]

**Trigger 3: When user abandons session after 5+ minutes**
- Message: "Wait—unlock [feature] before you go. [X% off]. [CTA: Upgrade]"
- Timing: Exit intent
- Expected conversion: 5-8%[5]

**Key insight:** In-app prompts convert higher than email because they're contextual and immediate.[5]

**CHANNEL 3: PERSONALIZED DMs / SMS (Virality + Engagement)**[6]

**Tactic 3a: Cheeky, Timed DMs (Days 5-10)**[6]

**SMS/DM 1 (Day 5, 11 PM): "u up?" style message**

Message: "u up? premium is up to 40% off. better sleep with premium features 😴 [link]"

**Why this works:**[6]
- Timed for late-night scrollers (when they're most receptive)[6]
- Playful tone (matches Gen Z/millennial communication)[6]
- Casual language (not corporate)[6]
- FOMO + urgency (limited time)[6]

**Send to:** Younger segments (Gen Z, millennials) who engage with casual communication[6]

**SMS/DM 2 (Day 8, 2 PM): Reminder with different angle**

Message: "last 48h to upgrade ⏰ [X% off] [link]"

**Why this works:**[6]
- Different time (afternoon vs. late night)[6]
- Different message (urgency vs. casual)[6]
- Reaches different engagement patterns[6]

**Send to:** All segments[6]

**Key insight:** SMS/DM has 98% open rate. Use it strategically.[6]
