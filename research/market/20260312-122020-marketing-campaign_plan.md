### 2. TARGET AUDIENCE (SPECIFIC)

**Primary: Top 10 Customers from Validation Phase**

**Selection Criteria:**[1]
- ✅ Highest lifetime value (LTV) - customers who've spent most
- ✅ Most engaged - opened emails, used product frequently, positive interactions
- ✅ Best fit for advocacy - already promoting you organically
- ✅ Active social media presence - identifiable on LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok[1]
- ✅ Strong audience engagement - not just follower count, but actual engagement rates[2]

**Profile:**
- Already experienced your product's benefits firsthand[1]
- Can authentically promote to their networks[1]
- Have credibility in their audience[1]
- Most likely to say yes (warm audience)[1]

**Why this audience:** Existing customers make ideal affiliates because they've already validated the product. 70% of affiliate revenue comes from outbound recruitment to warm audiences.[1]

### 3. CHANNELS AND TACTICS

**CHANNEL 1: PERSONALIZED EMAIL OUTREACH (Days 1-10)**

**Tactic 1a: Research & Personalization (Days 1-3)**[1]

Before sending any email, research each of your top 10 customers:[1]
- [ ] Review their website (if they have one)
- [ ] Check their social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram)
- [ ] Review previous interactions with your product
- [ ] Note specific content they've created or shared
- [ ] Identify their audience/network
- [ ] Find one specific thing to reference in your email[1]

**Example:** "I noticed you shared [specific post] about [topic] with your audience. That's exactly the problem we solve."

**Tactic 1b: Personalized Outreach Email (Days 4-6)**[1]

**Email Template (Highly Personalized):**

Subject: "[Name], we want to work with you as an affiliate"

Body:

"Hi [Name],

I was looking at [specific thing you noticed about them] and realized something: you're already solving [problem] for your audience. We built [product] to do exactly that.

You've been one of our best customers since [date], and I think you'd be perfect as an affiliate partner.

Here's the idea:

**You promote [product] to your audience (however you want—email, social, word of mouth).** For every customer who signs up through your unique link, you earn **20% commission per referral.**

**What you get:**
- 20% commission on every referral (no cap)
- Unique referral link + tracking dashboard
- Marketing materials (email templates, social graphics, landing page copy)
- Early access to new features
- Recognition as a top affiliate

**How it works:**
1. You get a unique affiliate link (e.g., affiliate.html?ref=[yourname])
2. You share it however you want
3. We track conversions from your link
4. You get paid monthly

No minimum sales required. No pressure. Just promote if and when you want.

Interested? Check out our affiliate program here: [affiliate.html link]

Or reply to this email and let's chat.

Thanks for being such a great customer.

[Your name]"

**Key elements from research:**[1]
- Personalized opening (reference their specific content/audience)
- Lead with benefits (commission rate, exclusive perks)
- Highlight mutual value[1]
- Open a conversation, don't overwhelm[1]
- Make it easy to say yes[1]

**Send to:** Top 10 customers (Days 4-6)
