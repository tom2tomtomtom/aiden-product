## STEP 1: CONFIRM YOUR WINNER IS PROFITABLE

**Before scaling, verify:**
- CPA is below your target (e.g., $25)[1][3][5]
- Frequency is stable (<3)[1][3]
- You have at least 50 conversions in learning phase[1][3][5]

**Campaign structure:**
- Move winner to dedicated "Scale" campaign[1]
- Keep testing and retargeting separate[1]
- Refresh creatives weekly to combat fatigue[2][5]

---

## STEP 2: VERTICAL SCALING (Budget Increases)

**Goal:** Reach ~$267/day ($8,000/month)[1][3][5]

**Under $500/day spend:**
- Increase by 20-25% every 24-48 hours[1][3]
- OR use automated rule: auto-increase 15% daily if CPA stays below target[1]
- Monitor: CPA stable, frequency <3[1][3]

**Example progression:**
$50/day → $60 → $75 → $100 (over 2-4 weeks)[5]

**$500+/day spend:**
- Jump by $100-500 increments every 3 days[3][5]
- ONLY with fresh creatives and >50 conversions[3][5]
- Monitor: Frequency >3, CPA spikes, ROAS drops[4][5]

**Critical rule:**
- Avoid >30% jumps or overnight doubles[3][4]
- This prevents learning phase resets and performance crashes[3][4]

| Spend Level | Increment | Frequency | Key Check |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| <$500/day | 20-25% | Every 24-48 hrs | CPA stable, frequency <3[1][3] |
| $500+/day | $100-500 | Every 3 days | 50+ conversions, new creatives[5] |

## STEP 3: HORIZONTAL SCALING (Expand Reach)

**When to start:** Once vertical scaling hits audience saturation[1][2][6]

**Method: Duplicate ad sets**
- Clone 2-3x with new audiences[1][2][6]
- Target: Broader lookalikes (2-5%), new interests, new geos, or full broad (USA, 25-55)[1][2][6]
- Reset clones to low budgets ($25-50/day) and scale independently[3]

**Alternative: Advantage+ campaigns**
- Launch new campaigns with winning creative[1]
- Use wide targeting; Meta's AI optimizes with pixel data[1]

**Persona expansion:**
- Test 3-5 custom audience variations[2][5]
- Goal: Distribute $8k across 3-5 ad sets/campaigns[1][3]

## MONITORING & RISK MANAGEMENT

**Track by angle, not just ad:**
- Price variant + creative + audience[2]
- Cut losers immediately[2]

**Watch for red flags:**
- Frequency >3[4][5]
- CPA spikes[4][5]
- ROAS drop[4][5]
- Pause and refresh if any occur[4][5]

**If stuck scaling:**
- Add top-of-funnel broad campaigns to build awareness[4][6]
