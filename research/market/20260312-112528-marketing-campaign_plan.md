## CAMPAIGN PLAN: Template + 1-on-1 Onboarding Call – $400 Meta Test

---

## 1. CAMPAIGN CONCEPT
**"Template + expert setup call – $89. No guessing. Just done."** – Position the premium tier as the friction-free path for freelancers who want the template *and* personalized guidance to implement it immediately.

---

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary:** Freelance writers and solopreneurs (ages 25-45) who:
- Create content or proposals regularly (pain point: time-consuming, inconsistent process)
- Have purchased or expressed interest in templates/systems
- Want done-with-you guidance, not just a template
- Are active on Instagram and Facebook
- Income: $40k-$150k annually
- Pain point: "I need the template AND someone to help me set it up right"

**Audience Targeting Parameters (Meta):**
- Interest stacks: "Freelance writing" + "Solopreneur" + "Content creation" + "Small business"
- Secondary interests: "Digital nomads," "Productivity," "Business tools"
- Age: 25-45
- Devices: Mobile-preferred
- Placements: Instagram Feed, Stories, Facebook Feed
- Lookalike: Create lookalike from existing customer list (if available)
- Exclusions: Recent converters (past 30 days), customers who already purchased $89 tier
