- What's your current conversion rate by channel?

- Which channels are you confident will scale?

**5. What's your actual budget?**
- How much are you allocating to paid acquisition?
- Over what timeframe?
- What's your expected ROI threshold?

**6. What's your actual goal?**
- Revenue target?
- Customer acquisition target?
- Market penetration goal?
- Timeline?
