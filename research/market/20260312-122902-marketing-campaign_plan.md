### 2. TARGET AUDIENCE (SPECIFIC)

**Primary: Existing $89/Month Basic Tier Customers**

**Segmentation by upgrade potential:**[1][6]

**Segment 1: High-Usage Power Users (30% of base)**
- Logged in 4+ times/week
- Using advanced features frequently
- High engagement with product
- **Why:** Most ready to upgrade, see clear value in premium features[1]
- **Messaging focus:** "Unlock unlimited features you're already using"

**Segment 2: Mid-Tenure Customers (40% of base)**
- Customers for 3-12 months
- Stable usage patterns
- Proven loyalty
- **Why:** Understand product value, less churn risk[1]
- **Messaging focus:** "Grow with us to the next level"

**Segment 3: Long-Term Customers (20% of base)**
- Customers for 12+ months
- Highest LTV, lowest churn
- Advocates/referrers
- **Why:** Most valuable, highest upgrade conversion[1]
- **Messaging focus:** "You've been with us since day one. Here's what's next."

**Segment 4: Lapsed/At-Risk Users (10% of base)**
- Haven't logged in 30+ days
- Previously engaged
- Churn risk
- **Why:** Win-back opportunity with upgrade incentive[1]
- **Messaging focus:** "We've added premium features. Come back and try them."

**Key insight:** Don't send same offer to all. Personalize by usage and tenure.[1][6]
