## CONVERSION TRACKING FRAMEWORK: Days 21-25

**Three metrics you need to track:**

### 1. CONVERSION RATE
**Formula:** (Number of Conversions ÷ Number of Sessions or Clicks) × 100[7]

**What it measures:** How efficiently traffic converts to acquisitions[7]

**Example from search results:**
- $10 variant: 50 conversions ÷ 2,000 sessions = 2.5%[1][4][5]
- $20 variant: 60 conversions ÷ 2,500 sessions = 2.4%[1][4][5]

---

### 2. COST PER ACQUISITION (CPA)
**Formula:** Total Campaign Cost ÷ Number of Conversions[1][2][4][6]

**What it measures:** How much you're spending to acquire each customer[1][2][4][6]

**Example from search results:**
- $10 variant: $1,000 cost ÷ 50 conversions = $20 CPA[1][4][5]
- $20 variant: $1,500 cost ÷ 60 conversions = $25 CPA[1][4][5]

---

### 3. REVENUE PER ACQUISITION (RPA)
**Formula:** Total Revenue from Conversions ÷ Number of Conversions[1]

**What it measures:** Average revenue generated per acquired customer[1]

**Example from search results:**
- $10 variant: $750 revenue ÷ 50 conversions = $15 RPA[1][4][5]
- $20 variant: $1,500 revenue ÷ 60 conversions = $25 RPA[1][4][5]

## HOW TO TRACK (Days 21-25)

**Step 1: Segment Your Data**
- Filter your dashboard for days 21-25[2][8]
- Group by price variant ($49, $89, $129)[2][8]
- Use UTM parameters or ad creative tags to distinguish variants[2][8]

**Step 2: Gather Required Metrics**
| Metric | Data Needed | Source |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Total Cost | Ad spend per variant | Platform reports[1][5] |
| Sessions/Clicks | Traffic volume per variant | Analytics[2] |
| Conversions | Purchases per variant (use post-conversion pixel) | Platform[1][2][4] |
| Revenue | Sales value per variant | E-commerce/CRM[1] |

**Step 3: Calculate per Variant**
Apply the three formulas above to each price point[1][4][5]

**Step 4: Validate Tracking**
- Test pixels/tags with Google Tag Assistant[1][2][3]
- Cross-check attribution models (e.g., last-click)[1][2][3]

**Step 5: Analyze & Optimize**
- Compare variants: lower CPA/higher RPA = winners[1][7]
- Set alerts for deviations[1][7]
