### PHASE 3: ACCELERATION & FOLLOW-UP (Days 26-40)

**Step 1: Leverage Early Wins (Days 26-30)**

**Capture success stories from first recruits:**[4]
- How much have they earned so far?
- How many sales have they made?
- What's their feedback on the program?
- Can they share a testimonial?

**Create affiliate spotlight:**[4]
- "Meet [Affiliate Name], who's already earned $[X] in commissions"
- Share their story (how they're promoting, what they're saying)
- Show their earnings example
- Use this in follow-up emails to remaining prospects

**Step 2: Build Community Momentum (Days 26-35)**

**Create private affiliate group:**[4]
- Platform: Facebook Group, Slack, or Discord
- Purpose: Share experiences, ask questions, celebrate wins
- Content: Tips for promoting, earnings updates, exclusive announcements
- Benefit: Reduces dropout, builds loyalty, encourages participation[4]

**Invite all recruits to group:**[4]
- Send welcome message
- Share first affiliate spotlight
- Ask them to introduce themselves
- Create sense of community

**Step 3: Persistent Follow-Up (Days 26-40)**

**Multi-touch follow-up sequence:**[7]

**Follow-up 1 (Day 26):** For non-responders to initial email
- Subject: "Quick question about the affiliate opportunity"
- Message: Softer ask, ask if they have questions, offer to chat
- Expected response: 10-15%

**Follow-up 2 (Day 32):** For still non-responders
- Subject: "[Name], [Affiliate 1] just earned $[X] in commissions"
- Message: Social proof from early recruit, show what's possible
- Expected response: 5-10%

**Follow-up 3 (Day 38):** Final push
- Subject: "Last chance: Join our affiliate program (closes [date])"
- Message: Urgency, show momentum, make final ask
- Expected response: 5-10%

**Key insight:**[7] Affiliates need multiple touchpoints before committing (like customers do). Don't give up after one email.
