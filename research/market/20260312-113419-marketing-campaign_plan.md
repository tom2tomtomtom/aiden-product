## EMAIL CAMPAIGN: Promote $19/Month Subscription to Existing Customers

---

## 1. CAMPAIGN CONCEPT

**"Convert one-time buyers into recurring revenue by positioning the subscription as the natural next step for customers who already trust us."**

---

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE

**Existing customers who have purchased:**
- Templates (one-time purchase)
- Setup calls ($199 or $89 service)

**Psychographic profile:**
- Already validated the product (they paid)
- Understand the value (they used it)
- Likely need ongoing support/resources
- Price-sensitive but quality-conscious (they bought once, not subscribed)

**Segmentation:**
- **Segment A:** Template buyers only (lower commitment, easier upsell)
- **Segment B:** Setup call participants (higher commitment, more likely to subscribe)

**Send to:** All existing customers. Segment B gets priority (they're warmer leads).

---

## 3. CHANNELS & TACTICS

| Channel | Tactic | Timing |
|---------|--------|--------|
| **Email** | 3-email sequence (value → urgency → final push) | Day 1, Day 3, Day 7 |
| **Landing Page** | Dedicated subscription page with 50% off offer | Live before Email 1 |
| **UTM Tracking** | `?offer=existing50` in all email links | All 3 emails |
| **Segmentation** | Send to Segment B first (setup call participants), then Segment A | Staggered by 1 day |

**Why email?** Existing customers already trust you. Email is direct, personal, and trackable. No need for paid ads here—you own this audience.

## 6. SUCCESS METRICS

### PRIMARY METRICS (Track Daily):

| Metric | Target | Action if Below Target |
|--------|--------|------------------------|
| **Email Open Rate** | >25% | A/B test subject lines; check send time |
| **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | >5% | Simplify CTA; test button color on landing page |
| **Conversion Rate** | >2% (of clickers) | Reduce landing page friction; add FAQ; test offer (maybe 60% off?) |

### SECONDARY METRICS:

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | If >0.5%, messaging may be too aggressive |
| **Landing Page Bounce Rate** | If >50%, page copy/design needs work |
| **Time to Conversion** | If >3 days, urgency messaging isn't working |
| **Subscription Churn Rate** | If >20% in first month, product isn't delivering on promise |
